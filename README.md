# Photonic Universe Hypothesis(PUH)

*A plain-language introduction to the framework, in its own terms. About fifteen minutes.*

---

## The claim

The Photonic Universe Hypothesis (PUH) proposes that everything — space, time, matter, gravity, the whole cosmos — is built from **high-energy photons folded into an E8 lattice**.

Not light as a thing moving *through* space. Light as the thing space is *made of*. When photons fold into the E8 lattice — an exceptional eight-dimensional structure from pure mathematics — that folding is what produces particles, mass, and spacetime itself.

The universe did not begin at the Big Bang. The Big Bang was a **rebound event**, the latest in an eternal cyclical series, and this framework sets out to describe the whole cycle: where a universe comes from, what sits at the centre of every massive body, how matter and antimatter were separated, and how it all eventually returns to light and begins again.

---

## The field everything comes from

Underneath everything is the **Super Polariton Field (SPF)** — an infinite field of E8 geometry. A polariton is a real, known state of physics: light and matter coupled into a single thing. The SPF is that principle taken to its foundation: an eternal medium out of which universes are born.

The SPF is the multiverse. A single photon field powers all of it. Individual universes are rebound events within it — and ours is one of them.

---

## The rebound event

A universe begins when a massive **Planck Star** embedded in the SPF rebounds.

The rebound is **Z3-symmetric** — a three-fold symmetry that splits the released energy into three equal parts:

- a **matter universe**,
- an **antimatter universe**,
- and a **throat** of pure energy between them that keeps the two apart.

This is why our universe is made of matter and not antimatter. They were separated at the rebound — carried apart by **two asymmetric rotating polar jets**, one for each — into distinct universes. The long-standing puzzle of "where did all the antimatter go" has a direct answer here: it is in the twin universe on the other side of the throat.

The rebound also drives a **huge magnetic field** and the jet structure that seeds what follows. And a tiny asymmetry in the rebound — a deviation from perfect three-fold symmetry — is what tips the balance and fixes the small matter–antimatter imbalance our universe was born with.

## Planck cores

At the centre of every gravitationally bound body — every black hole, every star, every planet — sits a **Planck core**.

This is where PUH parts company most sharply with standard physics. Conventional theory predicts a **singularity** at the heart of a black hole: infinite density in zero volume, the point where the equations break. PUH does not permit that. The E8 lattice can only fold so far. What sits at the centre is finite — extraordinarily dense, but bounded, and describable.

And these cores are not confined to black holes. The rebound seeded the early universe with **primordial Planck cores**, and matter gathered around them. Stars and planets formed around these seeds. **This one included** — the Earth is proposed to carry a primordial Planck core at its centre, a survivor of the previous cycle, and to have carried it for four and a half billion years.

**Planck cores do not evaporate.** This directly contradicts Hawking's prediction that black holes slowly radiate away and disappear. In PUH, the outer boundary of the core — the **Planck Shell** — is a perfect reflector: radiation is not emitted to infinity, it is reflected back. Cores do not shrink and vanish. They persist, and they can grow.

## The architecture of a core

A Planck core is a structured object with an **inner boundary** and an **outer boundary**, held together by **E8 lattice tension** — the same tension that, elsewhere, we experience as gravity.

That is the deep move in this framework: **gravity is E8 lattice tension, not spacetime curvature.** Where the lattice is folded and packed, tension is high, and matter responds to it. What Einstein described as curved spacetime, PUH describes as the tension field of a folded lattice. Gravity is not fundamental — it emerges from the packing.

## How structure forms: branched flow

The early universe was not smooth by accident. As matter streamed outward from the rebound through the E8 lattice, it underwent **branched flow** — the effect, confirmed in laboratory experiments, by which anything travelling through a slightly uneven medium organises into branching rivers rather than spreading evenly or scattering at random.

At cosmic scale, this is what built the **cosmic web**: the filaments, walls, and voids that galaxies are arranged along. The web is the branched-flow pattern of the primordial matter stream through the lattice, further shaped by **E8 lattice blocks** — the large-scale structure of the lattice itself. Galaxies did not scatter randomly. They condensed along the caustics of a directed flow.

## Amalgamation and the end of a cycle

Cores do not only form. They **merge** — a process called **amalgamation**, and it is proven to run one way only. Two cores can combine into one; a core can never split or evaporate. Merging is allowed, division is topologically forbidden. Over cosmic time, cores amalgamate into ever-larger cores.

This sets the ultimate fate of a universe. Gravity — lattice tension — slowly pulls spacetime back into the cores. Over **trillions upon trillions of years**, matter unfolds back into the photons it was made from, and those photons are drawn into a final, all-consuming **Amalgamated Planck Star**. Spacetime is pulled back completely. Every photon is recovered.

And then it waits. The final core sits for trillions of years — until the next **rebound event**. The recovered photons power the next universe. Nothing is lost. **The photon powers the entire multiverse**, cycle after cycle, forever.

This is a genuinely **cyclical universe**: rebound, expansion, amalgamation, pull-back, rebound again. No beginning, no true end — a breath rather than a firework.

## What this framework explains

Several long-standing problems dissolve rather than needing to be solved.

**The end of fine-tuning.** The constants of our universe are not delicately balanced by luck. They follow from the symmetry of the rebound. The Koide relation — a precise pattern in the electron, muon, and tau masses, unexplained for forty years — follows from the rebound's Z3 symmetry in three lines of algebra.

**Ultimate energy conservation.** Nothing is created or destroyed across cycles. The same photons, folded and unfolded, run the entire multiverse eternally.

**The smoothness of the early universe.** Not a coincidence to be explained away, but a consequence of the rebound and the lattice structure.

**And a chain of emergence** that reorders what we usually take as fundamental:

- **Mass creates time.** Time is not a stage that things happen on. It is produced by mass. Linear excitations of the lattice — pure light — carry no time; only folded, massive structure does.
- **Time requires mass.** No mass, no time. The two arrive together.
- **Mass and time make entropy.** The arrow of time and the growth of disorder follow from the existence of mass in the lattice.

So **time, gravity, and entropy are all emergent properties** — not fundamental ingredients of reality, but consequences of photons folding into the E8 lattice. Mass is the root; time, gravity, and entropy grow from it.

## Why this is offered as science

The framework makes commitments that could prove it wrong, registered in advance:

- A required relationship between the sizes of cosmic voids and the spacing of the filaments between them, testable against current sky surveys.
- A predicted flattening in the mass–radius relation of stars, at the scale set by the primordial core population.
- A floor on the mass of the smallest primordial cores, which existing observations already constrain.
- Predictions from the rebound's Z3 symmetry, testable by DUNE, LIGO, Hyper-Kamiokande, and DESI within years.

The method matters as much as the claims. Falsification conditions are written down and dated *before* the data is examined. Where earlier work is wrong, later work corrects it by number and section, on the record. Results from other sources — including AI systems — are treated as leads to verify, never as established facts.

The principle governing all of it:

> **Truth comes with mistakes — as long as we catch them early.**
